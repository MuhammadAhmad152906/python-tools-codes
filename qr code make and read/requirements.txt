?? Required Libraries

Here are the specific third-party libraries used in your script:


pillow: For handling, resizing, and rendering images (PIL.Image, PIL.ImageTk).


qrcode: For generating standard QR codes.


python-barcode: For generating Code 128 barcodes.


segno: For generating Data Matrix codes.

::cmd command::

pip install pillow qrcode python-barcode segno
